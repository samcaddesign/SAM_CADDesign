We are SAM Groups

SAM CADDesign is the custom based CAD Service Firm working towards excellance of the primary business in "Design & Analysis" through innovation and technology. SAM CADDesign, one of the contributions of SAM Groups. It has established a highly regarded position of being the industry leader in providing CAD modelling projects [Especially in SOLIDWORKS, CATIA, CREO, AUTODESK INVENTOR ] and simulation design support, 2D Drafting [Autocad], CNC Programming, CAD training and functional mentoring. We have developed modelling projects for various industries, including automotive, aerospace, medical and for customers innovation.

Why our needs

Customers will outsource all or any aspect(s) of their design to our highly experienced team. We help customers break through their design challenges by providing industry leading solutions that speed product development into volume production. Our team leading critical skills include complete project management capabilities, extensive manufacturing know-how and our own exclusive Parametric CAD design process that allows us to make quick modeling changes without extensive remodeling or unexpected downtime.

Our Team possesses industry leading critical skills and our firm boasts the highest work force retention rate in the industry.

In addition to CAD/CAM modelling and simulation, we support all other major CAD systems along with Analysis Packages [ANSYS, ABACUS, Altair HYPERMESH].
