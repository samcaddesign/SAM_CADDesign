AutoCAD


AutoCAD is a commercial computer-aided design (CAD) and drafting software application. Developed and marketed by Autodesk. Before AutoCAD was introduced, most commercial CAD programs ran on mainframe computers or minicomputers, with each CAD operator (user) working at a separate graphics terminal.

AutoCAD is used in industry, by architects, project managers, engineers, graphic designers, city planners and other professionals. It is a mechanical design software used for manufacturing. AutoCAD Mechanical engineering design software which is used for manufacturing products for acceleration of mechanical CAD process in AutoCAD environment.
