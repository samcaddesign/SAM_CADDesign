SOLIDWORKS


SOLIDWORKS is a solid modeling computer-aided design (CAD) and computer-aided engineering (CAE) computer program that runs primarily on Microsoft Windows. While it is possible to run SolidWorks on MacOS, It is not supported by SolidWorks. SolidWorks is published by Dassault Syst√®mes.

SolidWorks transports all tasks concerned for product development into a visual and electronic 3D world for enhanced collaboration. With its insightful interface, and a broad range of expertise and compatible tools to support all drawing and engineering functions, SolidWorks assures to:

  • Shortened design cycle
  • Increased productivity of engineers and designers
  • Faster delivery of innovative products

3D modeling is basically preferred by all students, engineers and designers because they offer various advantages/facilities over the traditional 2D design. Hence, learning 3D CAD and SolidWorks became must because these are one of the most widely used 3D CAD tools in the industry. SolidWorks helps in conveying innovative ideas into designs very quickly, and effectively.

Importantly, anyone (engineer or designer) without having any prior experience in animation can produce an animated movie of any 3D product design instantly. This will help the engineer or designer to communicate their product ideas better to a non-technical person.


Features & Objectives :

  • CAD Animation: To create a 3D, animated movie of any product design
  • Product Data Management: To keep, reuse, repurpose all design data in a secure way
  • Revision Control: To keep track of the changes made to evolving product design, and monitor changes, each revision bring done to overall design and manufacturing cost
  • Costing: To check the design against manufacturing cost, and how to automatically generate manufacturing cost, as it is designed
  • Part and Assembly: To design parts and assemblies of parts
  • Tolerance Analysis: To analyze the tolerance levels of each individual part separately, as well as all that of parts when functioning collectively
  • CAD Library: To increase productivity by making use of CAD library
  • Interference Check: To check the way all parts fit among themselves, and operate as intended
